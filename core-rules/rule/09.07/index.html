09.07 Fall-Back Move
x

Eligible If

Your unit is engaged.

Effect

Your unit moves as described in Moving (03).

Maximum Distance

Your unit’s M characteristic.

Before Moving

Select fall-back mode:

■ Ordered Retreat: If your unit is not battle-shocked, you can select this mode.
■ Desperate Escape: Otherwise, you must select this mode. Make a hazard roll for each model in your unit (06.03).

While Moving

■ Desperate Escape: Each model that is moved can be moved through enemy models.

After Moving

■ Your unit must be unengaged.
■ Until the end of the turn, unless otherwise stated, your unit is not eligible to shoot, declare a charge or start an action.
■ Desperate Escape: If your unit is not battle-shocked, you
must make a battle-shock roll for your unit (01.07).

FAQ

If a rule says a unit auto passes Desperate Escape tests does that mean it would auto pass the hazard rolls from a desperate escape move?
