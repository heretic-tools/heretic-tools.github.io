10.05 Assault Shooting
x

Eligible If

All of the following apply to your unit:

■ Unengaged and made an advance move this turn.
■ Has one or more [ASSAULT] weapons.

Effect

Your unit shoots as described in Making Attacks (04).

While Shooting

You can only select [ASSAULT] weapons to make attacks with.

After Shooting

Until the end of the phase, your unit is not eligible to start an action.
