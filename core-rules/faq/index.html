18.05 - Change to Emergency Disembark Move

WHILE MOVING: Set up each model in your unit:

■ Wholly within the set-up distance of that TRANSPORT, and as close as possible to that TRANSPORT.
■ Or: If the above is not possible while remaining unengaged, set up that model wholly within the set-up distance of that TRANSPORT, as close as possible to that TRANSPORT, and engaged.

Each model that still cannot be set up is destroyed.

FAQ

FAQ

When declaring the allocation order of groups in my unit I have more than one non-CHARACTER groups that contain a model with a missing wound, how must I select the order?

Those groups can be selected in an order of your choosing but must be in an order that makes them come before any non-CHARACTER groups that do not contain a model with a missing wound.

FAQ

What does the X" in Lone Operative X" mean?

The X is a placeholder for a number of inches that the ability might appear in a rule with, like Lone Operative 15" where the X has been replaced with 15"

FAQ

If a rule says a unit auto passes Desperate Escape tests does that mean it would auto pass the hazard rolls from a desperate escape move?

Yes

FAQ

If a unit has a rule that allows it to start an action when it is battle-shocked can it also continue doing that action if it becomes battle-shocked after starting an action?

Yes

FAQ

When selecting a target unit can the enemy model that is visible to my model and the enemy model that is in range of my model's weapon be different enemy models in the same unit?

Yes

FAQ

If a rule modifies how far a unit or model can move during a pile-in move or consolidation move does it also change the distance of the target units?

No

FAQ

How many mortals do you take from Hazardous tests for a combined vehicle/monster and Infantry unit

The roll would be all at once and it would be 1 MW per fail

FAQ

My rule says that "this units ranged attacks have +1 A", do each of my attacks get an extra attack dice?

No, it increases the A characteristics of the unit's ranged weapons by 1.

FAQ

I failed hazard rolls from [Hazardous] weapons, do those mortal wounds have to be allocated to the models with the [Hazardous] weapons?

No, you allocate the mortal wounds to the unit as per Mortal Wounds(06.02). (This represents Bob the guardsmen picking up his fallen squad mates plasma gun or that the twisting power of James the Chaos Sorcerer pulling his followers into the warp.)

FAQ

Can a model be within range of a weapon if it is not visible

Yes

FAQ

I have a unit being attacked with weapons that will modify some of their save rolls to be worse then 6 should I make those save rolls?

Yes, because other models in your unit may have Sv's that are better those models you've mentioned, this means that enough save rolls could be allocated to the those models that the rest of the results are not greater then the Sv's of the other models in your unit.

FAQ

If a rule says my unit must make a hazard roll does every model in the unit make a hazard roll?

No, the unit would make 1 single hazard roll.

FAQ

Do models destroyed when a unit is out of coherency count for mission objectives?

Yes.

FAQ

If a rule allows my unit to pile-in or consolidate an extra distance in the Fight phase, when can I use that rule?

For pile-in moves, during the Pile-in step(12.02), and for consolidation moves, during the Consolidate step(12.07) of the Fight phase.

FAQ

Can I select a unit to shoot or fight if it doesn't have melee or ranged weapons?

Yes, it just makes no attacks and then the fight type or shoot type is resolved.

FAQ

When checking coherency for my unit can my unit be in multiple groups?

No, it must be in a single contiguous group as per Units and Models (01.02) and What is Coherency in the core rules.

FAQ

If my unit has taken control of an objective at the end of my movement phase and that unit has as an ability that secures an objective they control, can they secure that objective?

Yes

FAQ

If my unit has the Deadly Demise ability and has an ability that allows it to Fight on Death, how do I resolve this?

Fight on Death rules are resolved before Deadly Demise abilities, meaning that your unit's Deadly Demise will resolve after your unit has fought/at the end of the phase and the relevant models have been removed from the battlefield.

FAQ

If I have a rule that says my unit can move up to 6" when it makes a pile-in move or consolidation move, does this also mean it changes the maximum distance of those moves to 6"?

Yes

FAQ

If a unit makes any disembark move in a turn (i.e. Disembark move, Emergency Disembark move etc.) has it disembarked that turn?

Yes.

FAQ

When a move says that a model or unit must move in a way if it is able, can that requirement force the unit to end their move not in coherency?

No, when a model or unit is making a move it must follow requirements while ending that move in coherency

FAQ

If the [ANTI] ability on my unit is ANTI-non-Monster/Vehicle does it mean the [ANTI] rule triggers when targeting units that do not have the Monster or Vehicle keyword?

Yes, ANTI-non-(any keyword) will trigger on any unit that does not have the specified keyword.

FAQ

If a unit becomes battle-shocked after starting an action does it stop performing that action?

Yes

FAQ

Aerialists ability allows you to get into a transport even though they disembarked a transport this turn?

Yes, as the unit embarking is not making a normal, advance or fall-back move as part of this embarkation.

FAQ

The Munitorium Field Manual shows my unit can attach to different units then are on it's datasheet, which do I use?

Use the Munitorium Field Manual.

FAQ

I have a rule that triggers when a mortal wound is allocated to a model, when does this happen?

When you select a model in the Select Model step of Mortal Wounds (06.02).

FAQ

Does my model or unit become engaged while moving through a enemy units engagement range during a move?

No

FAQ

If my unit has weapons that are identical in characteristics, but some of those weapons have [PRECISION] and those weapons targeted a unit without the character keyword, do I roll all of those weapons together?

Yes, the [PRECISION] ability is not an applicable ability for those attacks as there is no CHARACTER in the target unit for the ability to trigger its rule.

FAQ

If a rule says a unit can embark into a Transport in a turn that unit disembarked, does this ignore the core rules restriction on embarking in a turn that unit made a set up move (see 18.02)?

Yes

FAQ

When a rule instructs you to perform a charge outside of the charge phase, do I resolve step 2 and 3 of the Charge step(11.02)?

Yes
