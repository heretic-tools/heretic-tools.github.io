Lore

Wulfen exist on the brink of constant berserk rages, and their chilling howls unleash the inner beast in the sons of Russ nearby. They are bestial warriors, altered by the curse lying in the blood of all scions of the Wolf King. They are hunters, bounding forward with fangs bared and claws outstretched.
