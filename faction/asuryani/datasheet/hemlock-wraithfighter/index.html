Lore

Hemlocks blend their Spiritseer pilots’ psychic abilities with the gestalt energies of dead Aeldari, who can pilot the craft should the seer be incapacitated. The aircraft’s mindshock pod unleashes waves of negative emotion to cause terror in the enemy while its heavy D‐scythes hurl the Hemlock’s victims into the Warp.
