Lore

Wraithguard are all but impervious to pain; this is vitally important, for their D‐scythes and wraithcannons are short‐ranged and require them to advance close to the foe. These mighty weapons fire arcane beams that open a momentary rift between realspace and the Warp, capable of tearing any target apart.
