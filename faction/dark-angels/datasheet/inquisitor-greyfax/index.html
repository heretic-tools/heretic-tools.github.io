Lore

Inquisitor Greyfax is feared even by many in her own order, the Ordo Hereticus. Devoid of mercy or remorse, she is an iron‐willed warrior. She is also a powerful psyker, with telepathic abilities that enable her to detect lies in those she questions. She also wields an archaic but powerful condemnor‐pattern boltgun, which can shoot blessed stakes into heretics’ poisoned hearts.
