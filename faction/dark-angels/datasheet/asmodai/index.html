Unless otherwise stated, each bodyguard unit can only have one leader unit and one support unit attached to it.

Exemplar of Hate

Datasheet

While this model is leading a unit, each time a model in that unit makes a melee attack, you can re-roll the Hit roll.

Feared Interrogator

Datasheet

At the start of the Fight phase, each enemy CHARACTER unit within 6" of this model must take a Battle-shock test, subtracting 1 from that test when they do. In addition, each time this model destroys an enemy CHARACTER model with a melee attack, you gain 1CP.

Lore

Asmodai is the Dark Angels’ most successful Interrogator-Chaplain. Relentless and humourless, in battle he incites his battle-brothers’ fighting spirit to reach new heights, rendering them unstoppable killing machines by chanting litanies of hate with unshakeable belief.
