Deep Strike

Core

Each time this unit makes an ingress move (20.04), if every model in this unit has this ability, it can be set up anywhere on the battlefield that is more than 8" horizontally from all enemy units, even if that is within your opponent’s deployment zone.

There are many ways by which to deploy troops to the field of battle including tunnels, teleportation devices and other esoteric means of transportation.

Tunnelling Horrors

Datasheet

At the end of your opponent’s turn, if this unit is not within Engagement Range of one or more enemy units, you can remove this unit from the battlefield. In the Reinforcements step of your next Movement phase, set it up anywhere on the battlefield that is more than 8" horizontally away from all enemy models.

Lore

Repugnant to other Necrons, Ophydian Destroyers echo elements of servile Canoptek Wraiths and reviled Flayed Ones in their murder-optimised bodies. Tunnelling through solid ferrocrete with frenzied violence and flickers of dimensional displacement, they burst into view to ambush, hack and rend their prey apart.
