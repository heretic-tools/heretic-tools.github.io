Lore

A pilot must have immense faith for a Paragon Warsuit’s spirit to trust them. They must also have incredible skill to handle its control system, which allows for highly complex and fluid movements. Mastering a Paragon Warsuit makes a Sister capable of engaging the most fearsome enemies and emerging victorious.
