Lore

Celestian Insidiants are shadowy figures who specialise in hunting down witches and other heretics for capture or assassination. Employing cunning and skill to creep close to their foes, they explode suddenly into action amidst bellowed prayers and imprecations, hammering their prey with condemnor fire and cutting them down with blade and mace.
