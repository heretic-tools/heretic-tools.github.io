Leader

Core

Before the battle, in the Muster Armies step, for each leader and support unit in your army, you can select one friendly bodyguard unit that unit can lead. That unit will then lead that bodyguard unit for the battle and form an attached unit with it.

Unless otherwise stated, each bodyguard unit can only have one leader unit and one support unit attached to it.

Strategic Knowledge

Datasheet

While this model is leading a unit, that unit is eligible to shoot and declare a charge in a turn in which it Advanced or Fell Back.

Rites of Battle

Datasheet

Once per battle round, one unit from your army with this ability can use it when its unit is targeted with a Stratagem. If it does, reduce the CP cost of that use of that Stratagem by 1CP.

Lore

The galaxy’s foremost xenos hunters, each Watch Master commands one of the Deathwatch Chapter’s vigilant watch fortresses. These leaders possess centuries of strategic and esoteric knowledge of the horrors assailing Mankind. In battle, they wield potent vigil spears, employing their masterful skills to the fullest.
