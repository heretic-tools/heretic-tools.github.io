Deep Strike

Core

Each time this unit makes an ingress move (20.04), if every model in this unit has this ability, it can be set up anywhere on the battlefield that is more than 8" horizontally from all enemy units, even if that is within your opponent’s deployment zone.

There are many ways by which to deploy troops to the field of battle including tunnels, teleportation devices and other esoteric means of transportation.

Lore

When the foe puts their faith in sheer weight of numbers or seeks to bog the T’au cadres down with infantry waves, Crisis Starscythe Battlesuits provide a swift and devastating response. Leaping in close, their courageous pilots unleash the fury of blitzing burst cannons and roaring T’au flamers, reaping a macabre tally amongst their swarming foes before jetting back out of harm’s way.
