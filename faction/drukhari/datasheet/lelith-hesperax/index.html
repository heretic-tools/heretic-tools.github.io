Datasheet

Datasheet

Lore

Lelith Hesperax is the undisputed queen of the Commorrite arenas, a warrior capable of felling the mightiest armoured foes and most monstrous beasts with a pair of simple – if beautifully balanced – knives. Hesperax makes a spectacle of her every battle, dancing and weaving around her victims with mocking ease as she builds to a spectacularly gory crescendo.
