Leader

Core

Before the battle, in the Muster Armies step, for each leader and support unit in your army, you can select one friendly bodyguard unit that unit can lead. That unit will then lead that bodyguard unit for the battle and form an attached unit with it.

Unless otherwise stated, each bodyguard unit can only have one leader unit and one support unit attached to it.

Hatred Eternal

Datasheet

In your Shooting phase or the Fight phase, when you select this model’s unit to shoot or fight, you can spend 1 Pain token to Empower that unit. While that unit is Empowered, each time a model in that unit makes an attack, you can re‑roll the Hit roll.

Overlord

Datasheet

Once per battle, at the start of any phase, you can select one friendly DRUKHARI unit that is Battle‑shocked and within 12" of this model. That unit is no longer Battle‑shocked.

Devious Mastermind

Datasheet

Once per battle round, one model from your army with this ability can use it when its unit is targeted with a Stratagem. If it does, reduce the CP cost of that use of that Stratagem by 1CP.

Lore

Archons lead their kabals in battle with a mixture of malevolent cunning, arrogant self‑assuredness and breathtaking martial skill. To these qualities, they add an arsenal of esoteric weaponry and wargear that enable them to fight wreathed in concealing shadows, drag the souls from their victims’ bodies, and reduce enemies to desiccated ashes with a single blow.
