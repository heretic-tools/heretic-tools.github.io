Deep Strike

Core

Each time this unit makes an ingress move (20.04), if every model in this unit has this ability, it can be set up anywhere on the battlefield that is more than 8" horizontally from all enemy units, even if that is within your opponent’s deployment zone.

There are many ways by which to deploy troops to the field of battle including tunnels, teleportation devices and other esoteric means of transportation.

Slashing Dive

Datasheet

In your Movement phase, after this unit ends a Normal move, you can select one enemy unit it moved over during that move and roll one D6 for each model in this unit: for each 4+, that enemy unit suffers 1 mortal wound.

Lore

These predatory daemons strike like sudden and inescapable change. They emit unearthly shrieks as they swoop down upon their prey on scintillating tides of sorcery, slicing victims to ribbons with razor-keen fins and latching on with fanged jaws from which corrosive empyric energies flow.
