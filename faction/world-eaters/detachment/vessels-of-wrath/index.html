Lore

Every Khornate champion treads a personal Path to Glory. Through carnage and bloodshed, they earn empowering boons of martial might.

Enhancements

Archslaughterer

40 ptsMiniature

World Eaters model only. (Once per battle, per army) In your Command phase, you can use this ability. If you do, every Blessing of Khorne is active for this unit until the start of your next turn.

This gifted killer channels the fullness of his rage into every weapon swing – doubly so in that fleeting moment when he believes the Blood God’s gaze has fallen upon him.

With but a stroke of its infernal weapons, this favoured entity can hack paths through the meat of realspace itself to reach the foe all the quicker.

That World Eaters Character unit.

■ +1 A.

Punish the Craven

1 CP

The Blood God is said to detest cowards. Meting out punishment to such degenerates is believed to be a sure way to win his favour.

When

Your opponent's Movement phase, when a unit is selected to make a fall-back move, if that unit is engaged with a friendly World Eaters Character unit.

Target

That World Eaters Character unit.

Effect

When an enemy unit engaged with your unit is selected to make a fall-back move, that enemy unit must use the desperate escape mode. If that enemy unit is battle-shocked, -1 from those hazard rolls.

Scorn the Witch

1 CP

Those who feel Khorne’s blessings burning through their bodies and minds can shrug off hostile witchery with the force of their hate-fuelled contempt.

When

Any phase, when a friendly World Eaters Character unit (excluding EPIC HERO units) suffers a mortal wound.

Target

That World Eaters Character unit.

Effect

Your unit has Feel No Pain 4+ against mortal wounds.
