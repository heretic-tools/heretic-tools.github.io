Deep Strike

Core

Each time this unit makes an ingress move (20.04), if every model in this unit has this ability, it can be set up anywhere on the battlefield that is more than 8" horizontally from all enemy units, even if that is within your opponent’s deployment zone.

There are many ways by which to deploy troops to the field of battle including tunnels, teleportation devices and other esoteric means of transportation.

Bio-minefield

Datasheet

Enemy units cannot start or end an Advance move within 6" of this unit.

Floating Death

Datasheet

Each time this unit or an enemy unit ends a move, for each model in this unit that is within 3" of one or more enemy units, select one of those enemy units. That model in this unit is destroyed, then roll one D6: on a 2-5, that enemy unit suffers 1 mortal wound; on a 6, that enemy unit suffers D3 mortal wounds.

FAQ

Are Spore Mines units that are added to an army during a battle restricted by abilities that prevent Reserves from being set up within a certain range (e.g. Space Marines Infiltrators’ Omniscrambers)?

No.

Lore

Spore Mines are a form of living bomb, which drift across the battlefield in shoals and detonate when they detect prey organisms nearby. Those caught in the blast are torn apart as surely as if they had caught a brace of live frag grenades. The Hive Mind often uses Spore Mines to deny areas of the battlefield to the prey
