09.06 Advance Move
x

Eligible If

Your unit is on the battlefield and unengaged.

Effect

Your unit moves as described in Moving (03).

Maximum Distance

Advance roll + your unit’s M characteristic.

Before Moving

Make an advance roll by rolling one D6.

After Moving

■ Your unit must be unengaged.
■ Until the end of the turn, unless otherwise stated, your unit is not eligible to declare a charge or start an action.
