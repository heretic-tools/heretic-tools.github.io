Lore

Crusader Squads storm into battle with bolt rifles blazing and chainswords howling. Initiates aim jets of fire from their pyreblasters or swing crackling power fists into their foes, while hard-eyed Neophytes fight furiously to prove their martial worth under the stern gaze of their mentors.
