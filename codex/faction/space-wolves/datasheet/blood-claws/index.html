Lore

Young and fiery warriors full of belligerent enthusiasm, Blood Claws hunger to prove themselves in savage battle. They plunge headlong into the foe with relentless exuberance, straddling the line between sheer heroism and recklessness. Many great sagas start with the Blood Claws’ glory hunts.
