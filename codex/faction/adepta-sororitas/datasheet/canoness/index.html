Leader

Core

Before the battle, in the Muster Armies step, for each leader and support unit in your army, you can select one friendly bodyguard unit that unit can lead. That unit will then lead that bodyguard unit for the battle and form an attached unit with it.

Unless otherwise stated, each bodyguard unit can only have one leader unit and one support unit attached to it.

Sacred Command

Datasheet

Once per battle round, one unit from your army with this ability can use it when it is targeted with a Stratagem. If it does, reduce the CP cost of that usage of that Stratagem by 1CP.

The Emperor’s Grace

Datasheet

Once per battle, at the start of any phase, this model can use this ability. If it does, until the end of the phase, this model has a 2+ invulnerable save.

Lore

Martial and spiritual commanders of the Orders Militant, Canonesses are as much veteran warriors with decades of experience as they are shining examples of purity. Possessing impeccable leadership skills, tactical prowess and immense faith, Canonesses are beacons of both pious virtue and holy wrath.
