Lore

Haughty nobles whose value to the Imperium is huge, Navigators take to the battlefield only rarely, typically to complete some vital mission for their voidship’s master. At such times, their ability to perceive the shifting tides of the warp makes them powerful assets, as does the soul‐blasting gaze of their mutant third eye.
