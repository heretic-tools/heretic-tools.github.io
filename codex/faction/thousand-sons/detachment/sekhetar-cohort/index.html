Lore

Some amongst the sorcerers of the Thousand Sons have learned to channel their wills through the mechanical bodies of Sekhetar Robots, rendering the constructs extensions of their own subtle bodies.

Enhancements

Occulus Infernum

20 ptsMiniature

Sorcerer/Exalted Sorcerer model only. In your Movement phase, at the start or end of this unit’s move, you can select one friendly SEKHETAR ROBOTS unit within 6" of this unit. That unit’s ranged attacks have +1 BS until the start of your next turn.

It may be the Sekhetar Robots that fire upon the foe, but it is their sorcerous master that aims through their stolen senses.

Walking Rampart

30 ptsMiniature

Sorcerer/Exalted Sorcerer model only. This model has the following abilities:

■ Soul Bound: In your Movement phase, at the start or end of this unit’s move, you can select one friendly SEKHETAR ROBOTS unit within 3" of this model. That SEKHETAR ROBOTS unit heals D3+1 wounds.
■ Kine‑shielded Guardians: While this model is within 3" of a friendly SEKHETAR ROBOTS unit, this model has Lone Operative.

This Sorcerer callously employs the Sekhetar Robots as a mobile fortification, pouring sorcery into them to repair their battered forms and maintain his ambulatory shield.

Ectoplasmic Extrusion

1 CP

Employing a form of limited astral projection, the Sorcerer possesses these robots and then extrudes ectoplasmic limbs from within their bodies to work his will even as they continue to fight.

When

Your Shooting phase, when a friendly SEKHETAR ROBOTS unit within 12" of a friendly Thousand Sons Psyker unit starts an action.

Target

That SEKHETAR ROBOTS unit.

Effect

That action does not prevent your unit from being eligible to shoot.

Target

That SEKHETAR ROBOTS unit.

Effect
