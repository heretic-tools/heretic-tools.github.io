Stratagems

Prismatic Displacement

1 CP

Flickering light like cast through a many-faceted gem, the skyborne warriors of Tzeentch seem to manifest and fade, then leap back into sharp relief elsewhere with sudden violence.

When

Your Movement phase, when a friendly Infantry/Mounted Mutant unit is selected to make an advance/fall back move.

Target

That Infantry/Mounted Mutant unit.

Effect

■ Your unit’s ranged attacks have [ASSAULT] until the end of the turn.
■ That move does not prevent your unit from being eligible to shoot/declare a charge.

1 CP

Time runs differently around these warriors, allowing them ample space to act while their foes appear to lurch sluggishly on around them.

When

Your Movement phase, when a friendly Infantry/Mounted Mutant unit is selected to make an advance/fall back move.

Target

That Infantry/Mounted Mutant unit.

Effect

In a turn your unit made an advance/fall-back move, that move does not prevent your unit from being eligible to start an action.
