Lore

Voidsmen‐at‐Arms provide security on the ships of Rogue Traders and the Navis Imperialis alike. Well drilled and well equipped, they are highly adept at fighting in vessel corridors and other close‐quarters environments. They bring these skills to the battlefield amidst hails of rotor cannon fire and swift, decisive salvoes.
