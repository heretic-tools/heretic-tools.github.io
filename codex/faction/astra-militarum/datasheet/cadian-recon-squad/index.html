Infiltrators

Core

During deployment, if every model in a unit has this ability, it can be set up anywhere on the battlefield that is more than 8" horizontally from your opponent’s deployment zone and all enemy units.

Many armies employ reconnaissance units who can sit concealed, waiting for the right moment to strike.

Stealth

Core

If every model in a unit has this ability, each time a ranged attack targets that unit, that unit has the benefit of cover against that attack (13.08).

The stealthiest warriors can evade the attentions, and the bullets, of their foes.

Independent Operatives

Datasheet

Each time an Order is issued to this unit, that Order affects this unit until a new Order is issued to this unit, or until this unit become Battle‑shocked, or until the battle ends (whichever happens first).

Lore

Veteran soldiers who have lived long enough to receive additional training in stealth, reconnaissance and ambush, Cadian Recon Squads are an asset to any regiment. They get the best from the weapons they wield, display hard-won warrior cunning, and provide their commanding officers with a ready flow of strategic intelligence.
