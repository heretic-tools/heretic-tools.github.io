Deep Strike

Core

Each time this unit makes an ingress move (20.04), if every model in this unit has this ability, it can be set up anywhere on the battlefield that is more than 8" horizontally from all enemy units, even if that is within your opponent’s deployment zone.

There are many ways by which to deploy troops to the field of battle including tunnels, teleportation devices and other esoteric means of transportation.

Brass Stampede

Datasheet

Each time this unit ends a Charge move, select one enemy unit within Engagement Range of this unit and roll one D6 for each model in this unit: for each 4+, that enemy unit suffers D3 mortal wounds.

Lore

Unholy fusions of daemon and machine, Juggernauts of Khorne are unstoppable beasts whose blood is fire and whose every step is thunder. When ridden into battle by Bloodletters, they act as shock cavalry, bearing down on their victims like battering rams. The destruction when their charge hits home is hideous to behold.
