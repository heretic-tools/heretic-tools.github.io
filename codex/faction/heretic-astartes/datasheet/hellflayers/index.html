Deep Strike

Core

Each time this unit makes an ingress move (20.04), if every model in this unit has this ability, it can be set up anywhere on the battlefield that is more than 8" horizontally from all enemy units, even if that is within your opponent’s deployment zone.

There are many ways by which to deploy troops to the field of battle including tunnels, teleportation devices and other esoteric means of transportation.

Cutting Down the Foe

Datasheet

Each time a model in this unit makes a melee attack, if this unit made a Charge move this turn, improve the Strength and Damage characteristics of that attack by 1.

Lore

Mortal myth has it that the Hellflayers of Slaanesh began as devices for mowing the gruesome pleasure-gardens of the Dark Prince’s realm, but that their efficacy in war was soon proven when their wild Daemonette crews unleashed the whirl-bladed murder engines upon the battlefields of realspace.
