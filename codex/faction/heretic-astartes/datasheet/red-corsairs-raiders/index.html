Lore

Ruthless and cruel even by the standards of Renegade Space Marines, Red Corsairs Raiders excel in hit-and-run attacks. They fight with cunning brutality, maiming their victims to be taken as captives or looting dying foes even before their life blood has stopped jetting from their ragged wounds.
