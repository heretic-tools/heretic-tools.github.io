Lore

Deathwatch Veterans’ skills have been honed in their former Chapters for decades, sometimes centuries. Each battle‐brother brings their own talents and specialisms to the Long Vigil. Moreover, with access to a wide range of alien‐killing weaponry, they are able to act as elite shock troops against almost any foe.
