Lore

The Ravenwing Black Knights are the 2nd Company’s greatest warriors. They are elite fighters who style themselves after the monster-hunting knights of old Caliban. They speed towards the foe, swinging their corvus hammers with such force that the spiked weapons puncture the thickest armour and lay low the greatest prey.
