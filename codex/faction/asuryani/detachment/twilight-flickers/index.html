Lore

With their holofields attuned to a mist‑wreathed shadow performance, the Harlequins are insubstantial blurs as they dash through cover to reach their chosen stage.

Enhancements

Prelude Performer

20 ptsMiniature

Harlequins model only. This unit has Scouts 6".

With breathtakingly subtle yet rapid movements, this master of the opening act leads the Harlequins in a deadly dance that can catch their foes off guard.

Shadowfall Masks (Upgrade)

15 ptsUpgrade

Troupe unit only. This unit has Fights First.

These nightmarish masks torment the enemy with a psychoresponsive bombardment laced through the murk generated by the wearers’ holofields. The confusing distortion leaves enemies vulnerable to the blades that suddenly spear from the terrifying gloom.
