Lore

Jakhals are amongst the strongest and most bloodthirsty of the mortal followers of the World Eaters and seek to emulate their gore‐crazed masters in every way. When sent into battle, they enter the fray with wild abandon, hacking with their chain weapons in the hope of earning the favour of their lords.
