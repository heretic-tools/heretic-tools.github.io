Lore

Berzerkers of Khorne relish their role as the Blood God’s sacred destroyers and are fanatical in the extreme. The warp‐fuelled anger of these psychopathic warriors drives them into an endless frenzy of action. Those who face them in battle disappear under heavy blows, each potent enough to shear limbs and shatter shields.
