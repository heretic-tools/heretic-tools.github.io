Lore

These constructs serve as the eyes and ears of their masters while tomb worlds slumber, and as spectral assassins once their armies march out to war. Their dimensional destabilisation matrices allow them to phase in and out of reality, passing through solid objects and sliding their talons through armour, flesh and bone.
