Lore

Iron‑masters are amongst the most accomplished Brôkhyr of their guild. In battle, they maintain damaged war engines and mechanised soldiery, often aided by Ironkin and COG repair crews. These veteran Brôkhyr also bring their most powerful personal creations to war, taking satisfaction in unleashing them upon the foe.
