Lore

When a Brôkhyr hooks into a powerful exoframe, they become Thunderkyn. Adapted from rigs used to maintain Kin spacecraft, these armoured exoskeletons exchange repair tools for massive heavy weapons, which the Brôkhyr use to lay down withering covering fire for the oathbands and to pick off enemy armour.
