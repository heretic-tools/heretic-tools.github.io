Before the battle, in the Muster Armies step, for each leader and support unit in your army, you can select one friendly bodyguard unit that unit can lead. That unit will then lead that bodyguard unit for the battle and form an attached unit with it.

Unless otherwise stated, each bodyguard unit can only have one leader unit and one support unit attached to it.

Warped Interference

Datasheet / Psychic

While this model is leading a unit, each time a ranged attack targets that unit, models in it have the Benefit of Cover against that attack.

Wracking Agonies

Datasheet / Psychic

In your Shooting phase, after this model has shot, select one INFANTRY unit hit by one or more of those attacks made with its Agonising Energies. Until the start of your next turn, that unit is wracked with agonies. While a unit is wracked with agonies, subtract 2" from its Move characteristic and subtract 2 from Charge rolls made for it.

Lore

The Sorcerers of the Emperor’s Children delight in nothing more than toying with their foes. They utilise their fell magicks to inflict nightmarish agonies upon the minds and bodies of the living and to mask the advance of their underlings with illusory misdirection. They delight in each gruesome kill they make, for each is an offering to Slaanesh and promises a reward of power.
