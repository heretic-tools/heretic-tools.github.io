Lore

So certain are the Flawless Blades in their arrogant pre‑eminence, so sure they have already won before the first blow is struck, that they are filled with swift assurance that no foe can match.

Honour Is for Fools

1 CP

Far from facing enemy champions in fair duels one‑on‑one, the Flawless Blades fall upon them like a pack of jackals competing viciously to claim the glory of the kill.

When

Fight phase, when a friendly Flawless Blades unit is selected to fight.

Target

That Flawless Blades unit.

Effect

Your unit’s melee attacks have [Precision].

Intoxicated By Triumph

1 CP

Seeing their foes fleeing in terror before them only fires the chem‑laced blood of the Flawless Blades, urging them on toward their next challenge all the quicker.

When

Your opponent’s Movement phase, when an enemy unit that was engaged with a friendly Flawless Blades unit ends a fall‑back move, if that Flawless Blades unit is unengaged.

Target

That Flawless Blades unit.

Effect

Your unit can make a normal move of up to D3+3".
